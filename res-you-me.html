Res-You-Me Dashboard Design

Res-You-Me revolutionizes resume building by addressing common issues in existing platforms: limited font and color options, unattractive UIs, lack of live previews, and inflexible section arrangements. It offers diverse customizations, a modern UI with animations, real-time previews, and drag-and-drop section rearrangement for an enhanced user experience.
